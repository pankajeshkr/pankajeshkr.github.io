Transport Facilities

  • Buses
    Dehradun has a private bus service which
    you can use to get around the city.

  • Auto-rickshaws
    There are plenty of auto rickshaws around
    Dehradun but they don't have meters.

  • Walking
    The best way of getting around and exploring
    Dehradun is on foot. Considering Dehradun is
    a relatively condensed city it will be easy
    for you to find your way around.
