Transport Facilities

  • The best way
    The best way to reach Mussoorie from
    Roorkee is to take Dehradun Shtbdi from
    Roorkee to Dehradun then take a taxi from
    Dehradun to Mussoorie.

  • Buses
    You can take a taxi from Roorkee to
    Dehradun and then take State Transport Bus
    from Dehradun to Mussoorie.

  • Walking
    The best way to explore Mussoorie is on
    foot, as most of the religious attractions in
    the city are located in close proximity to each other.
