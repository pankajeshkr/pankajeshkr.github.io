Transport Facilities

  • The best way
    The best way to reach Nainital from Roorkee
    is to take Gangasatluj Exp from Roorkee to
    Seohara then take a taxi from Seohara to Nainital.

  • Taxi
    Taxis are available throughout the day for
    sightseeing tours. One important point to
    keep in mind here is that vehicles are not allowed on the Mall Road from 6 pm to 9 pm.

  • Walking
    The best way to explore Nainital is on
    foot, as most of the religious attractions in
    the city are located in close proximity to each other.
